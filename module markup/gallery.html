Our Programs

Madres Walking to Health:This grant project develops a sustainable community health program for the parents and their children at Washington Elementary School.

Abriendo Puertas: Opening Doors to Early Childhood Education Via iPads: This grant project works with five parents in the Greater Washington community of San Jose to support math and literacy development among children between the ages of two and five.

Adult Education Center/Centro Para Educacion de Adultos: The grant project provides a place for ongoing discussions and reflections with a focus issues of identity, culture, faith, and community.

Resilient Families Project: This grant project provides a series of workshops for families in the Greater Washington neighborhood that will support parents in forming stronger relationships with their children means to combat gang pressures.
