Santa Clara University graduates are in demand in Silicon Valley.

Did you know?

  • More than 5,000 employers recruit SCU students each year through the University’s Career Center.
  • When asked if college graduates were ready to work — 87% of companies that hire SCU graduates say yes, compared to 42% nationally.
  • 86% of SCU employers say SCU graduates demonstrate an excellent work ethic.
  • The top five employers of SCU graduates are Cisco, Apple, Google, Oracle, and Lockheed Martin.
